About

Raymond Etornam Agbeame is a Ghanaian computer programmer. He studied Computer Science at KNUST in Kumasi, Ghana. Professionally, he has worked in the San Francisco Bay Area for the Mozilla Corporation the now defunct company Rocketfuel Inc, CollectiveHealth Inc and Domino Data Lab.

He can be found on Twitter as the handle @retornam.
